# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and be open to feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on technical matters.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private
security advisory on GitHub, or contact the maintainers via the repository.
